Range Breakdown

Dive right in and observe the breakdown of two optimal ranges! This tool, when studied effectively, will allow you to instantly identify both your and your opponents' range, nut, strong pair, and strong draw advantages on any flop texture. The user will be able to select two ranges for any situation (e.g. button vs cutoff in a 3 bet pot) and input a flop to quickly break down both ranges, revealing which player has the advantage in what strength of hands.

Hand VS Range

Ever feel lost on exactly how to proceed when your opponent is betting his entire range for a small size on the flop? The 1/3 range c-bet strategy on specific board textures is a highly effective GTO simplification and is beginning to be employed more and more frequently even by lower stakes players. This tool is primarily built to develop a counter to said strategy by taking a look at which hands mathematically contain enough equity to continue vs a range c-bet.
