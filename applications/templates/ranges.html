Range Breakdown: How To Start

First pick which two positions you want to analyze. Next, create the flop that you want to evaluate! We will use our Monker-solved ranges to generate the EV of each player's range, along with breaking down the specific components that make up those portions. The results are now up to you to study as you please; hope you find them useful!

For terminology reference: SRP = single raised pot, 3BP = 3bet pot, and 4bp = 4bet pot. The first position displayed is the aggressor and the second is the caller (e.g. 3BP SB vs CO is a 3bet pot where SB has 3bet and CO has called). Check out our FAQ if you have questions about the range breakdown terminology! (I.e. " Top Pair+ " only refers to one pair hands only)
