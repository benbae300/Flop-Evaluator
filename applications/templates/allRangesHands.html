Hand vs Range: How To Start

This tool allows you to calculate the equity of one hand against an entire range on any given flop. First pick which two positions you want to analyze. Next, create the flop that you want to evaluate. Finally, select Hero's position and hand, and we will use our Monker-solved ranges to generate the equity of hero's hands against the given range. The results are now up to you to study as you please; hope you find them useful!

NOTE: the equities will take a few seconds to calculate! Please be patient! (For obvious reasons, SRPs take the longest).

For terminology reference: SRP = single raised pot, 3BP = 3bet pot, and 4bp = 4bet pot. The first position displayed is the aggressor and the second is the caller (e.g. 3BP SB vs CO is a 3bet pot where SB has 3bet and CO has called).
